The MOMOS Story

From a small family kitchen to your favorite momo restaurant

Humble Beginnings

It all started in 2008 in a small kitchen in Kathmandu. Our founder, inspired by generations of family recipes, began serving momos to neighbors and friends. The authentic taste and traditional cooking methods quickly made us a local favorite.

What began as a passion project soon turned into a mission to share the authentic taste of Nepal with the world.

Growing Together

As word spread about our authentic momos, we grew from a small kitchen to a beloved restaurant. Each expansion was guided by our core values: quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we continue to serve the same authentic recipes, now enjoyed by thousands of customers who have become part of our extended family.
