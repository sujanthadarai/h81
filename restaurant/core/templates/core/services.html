Common Questions

Service FAQ

What is the minimum notice required for catering?

We require at least 48 hours notice for small events (under 50 people) and 7 days for larger events. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.

Do you provide serving staff for catering events?

Yes, our premium and custom packages include professional serving staff. The basic package includes setup but not serving staff. Additional staff can be hired separately.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can customize menus for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. Please inform us of any restrictions when booking.

What is included in the private dining package?

Our private dining includes exclusive use of the space, dedicated server, customizable menu, basic decorations, and all amenities listed on the private dining section.

Are cooking workshops suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer workshops for all skill levels. Our momo making class is perfect for beginners, while our street food workshop is better for those with some cooking experience.

Do you offer corporate discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for corporate events and team-building workshops. Contact our corporate team for customized packages and pricing.
